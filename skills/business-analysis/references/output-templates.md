# Output Templates

## Template 1: Business Judgment

Use when the user asks "Is this worth doing?"

1. One-line conclusion
2. What the business actually is
3. Who pays and why
4. How the money likely comes in
5. Why it could work
6. Why it could fail
7. Fit for this founder/team
8. Final recommendation

## Template 2: Competitor Breakdown

Use when the user asks "How is this competitor making money?"

1. One-line conclusion
2. Business model
3. Traffic and acquisition clues
4. Offer and pricing clues
5. Revenue logic
6. Operational clues
7. What is worth copying
8. What is not worth copying
9. Confidence and unknowns

## Template 3: Multi-Option Ranking

Use when the user asks "Which direction should I choose?"

For each option:

1. What it is
2. Why it may work
3. Main risk
4. Best fit condition
5. Score

Then finish with:

1. Ranking
2. Recommended main path
3. Recommended backup path
4. First 30-day move
