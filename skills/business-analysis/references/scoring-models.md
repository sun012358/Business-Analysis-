# Scoring Models

Use one scoring model at a time. Do not mix weights unless the user asks.

## Model A: Opportunity Screening

Use when deciding whether a project or niche is worth doing.

- Demand clarity: 20
- Monetization clarity: 20
- Delivery lightness: 15
- Repeatability: 15
- Defensibility: 10
- Compounding potential: 10
- Founder fit: 10

Interpretation:

- 80-100: strong candidate
- 65-79: test lightly
- below 65: low priority

## Model B: Copyability

Use when judging whether a business is worth imitating.

- Audience can be reached: 20
- Offer can be replicated: 20
- Workflow can be learned: 15
- Distribution is realistically available: 20
- Founder-specific moat is low: 15
- Time-to-first-proof is reasonable: 10

Interpretation:

- 80-100: practical to imitate
- 65-79: partially copyable
- below 65: reference only

## Model C: Small-Team Fit

Use when the user wants lightweight, high-compounding businesses.

- Can be started without custom service: 20
- Can be run by 1-3 people: 20
- Low support burden: 15
- Can accumulate templates, content, data, or users: 20
- Clear pricing path: 15
- Low dependency on one founder's personality: 10

Interpretation:

- 80-100: strong small-team fit
- 65-79: workable with constraints
- below 65: likely too heavy
