# Validation Score Sheet

Use this score sheet when the user wants a more objective discussion.

## Scoring Scale

- 5: strongly validated
- 3: partially supported
- 1: doubtful
- 0: disproven

## Modules

### Demand /10

- Pain reality x2
- Willingness to act x3

High-risk assumption:
This is not a problem worth solving.

### Solution /10

- Value recognition x3
- Core behavior retention x2

High-risk assumption:
The proposed solution is not meaningfully better.

### Business Model /10

- Real willingness to pay x2
- Unit economics health x3

High-risk assumption:
The model cannot support itself.

### Growth /10

- Repeatable early channel x2
- CAC feasibility x3

High-risk assumption:
Users cannot be acquired sustainably.

### Defensibility /5

- Durable advantage x5

High-risk assumption:
The business can be copied too easily.

## Thresholds

- 36-45: green light, but only if demand and solution are both strong
- 24-35: yellow light, iterate and re-test weak modules
- 0-23: red light, reconsider or stop

## Project-Type Adjustment

### Small Cashflow Business

- Increase weight on business model and demand
- Lower weight on growth and defensibility
- Judge with cashflow realism

### Venture-Style Growth Business

- Increase weight on demand, growth, and defensibility
- Allow weaker short-term economics if the path is credible
- Judge with market scale and compounding potential
